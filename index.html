who is he

My name is Connor McGinnis and I’m equal parts human, musician, web developer, and gluten avoider. Since 2019 I’ve been swimming in the endless sea of web development, web design, and software engineering soaking up all I can.

I am a graduate of Burlington Code Academy located in beautiful Burlington, Vermont and haven’t skipped a day of coding since. When I’m not expanding my web development and design skills, I’m usually playing guitar, going for a run alongside Lake Champlain, being a subpar skateboarder, or planning my next adventure.

I’m currently available for freelance work, so feel free to reach out and tell me what we can partner on!
